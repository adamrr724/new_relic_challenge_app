Hello New Relic Team!

Thanks and welcome to my challenge application created using Node.js. Below you will find a mock support discussion between myself and a user (also myself :P) having trouble getting the New Relic Agent installed on their site.











My hope with this exercise is to give an accurate representation of a support conversation. Sometimes, small mistakes are made (like trying to require the New Relic agent in a .json file instead of in a .js file :)). It happens!

In my opinion, if you can approach the situation in a helpful, polite, and constructive way, New Relic customers will enjoy and appreciate the interaction. Be supportive, don't ever make them feel wrong or incapable, and be happy to provide the service. In short, always work to be on their team! Thanks again for giving me the chance to participate in this exercise. I thoroughly enjoyed this process, and I look forward to hearing from you soon!
